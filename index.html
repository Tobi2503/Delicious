Delicious Restaurant
Delicious opened on Thanksgiving Day 2023. Chef / Owner Tobi began baking pies and selling them to restaurants and his neighbors out of a small kitchen at the corner of Hudson and North Moore St. in Phnom Penh. Today, PP’s beloved restaurant and pie shop celebrates 1 years of classic, made from scratch Cambodia cooking. Delicious opened on Thanksgiving Day 2023. Chef / Owner Tobi. Located in Phnom Penh.

About Delicious Restaurant

Delicious opened on Thanksgiving Day 2023. Chef / Owner Tobi began baking pies and selling them to restaurants and his neighbors out of a small kitchen at the corner of Hudson and North Moore St. in Phnom Penh.

  • Excellent customer service.
  • Cambodian local restaurant serving.
  • Classic, made from scratch Cambodia cooking.

Tobi has been working in the restaurant industry for over 10 years, specializing in Cambodia cuisine. He has been passionate about creating a delicious and authentic Cambodian food experience for his customers.
